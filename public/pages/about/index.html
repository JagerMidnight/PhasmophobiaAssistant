About PhasmoMate

PhasmoMate is an unofficial fan-made website designed to help players of Phasmophobia with their ghost-hunting expeditions. It provides quick access to information about evidence, ghosts, and equipment to assist you in your spooky adventures.

PhasmoMate is not affiliated with or endorsed by Kinetic Games or Phasmophobia.
